ABOUT US

Discover new authors and genres with our unlimited book or audiobook rental service. Our members enjoy the highest commitment to customer service, satisfaction, and unmatched value. We help our members save time and money with the simplest and most convenient book rental service anywhere.

Launched in APRIL 2022, RENT READ RETURN.com is the newest online book rental service. Founded by Shivam Tiwari, Rent Read Return set out to revolutionize the way consumers acquired reading materials online. Oprah Magazine said It is the book version of Netflix. Originally launched as a paperback book rental service of bestselling paperback fiction books, it has expanded into children's books, nonfiction books, and audiobooks. Since 2000, It has grown its selection from 25,000 titles to over a quarter of a million titles. It donates overstock titles to Walter Reed Medical Center and is proud to support our wounded warriors. It also donates books to various libraries.
